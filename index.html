Melbourne food and Bar Scene

Everything you need to know about having a good time in Melbourne.

Foodie's Paradise

Now, let's talk about the food scene because Melbourne is a foodie's paradise! From hidden laneway cafes to bustling markets, there's something to tantalize every taste bud. Head to Queen Victoria Market for some fresh produce and artisanal treats, or dive into the multicultural culinary delights of Gertrude and Brunswick St Fitzroy or Smith St Collingwood. And don't even get me started on the laneway gems serving up everything from mouthwatering dumplings to Asian/Australian fusion. You'll be spoiled for choice, trust me!

Super Cool Bars

Hey there! So, let me tell you about Melbourne in all its glory! If you're into good music and love exploring microbreweries, Collingwood and Fitzroy are your jam. These neighbourhoods are bursting with indie breweries like Stomping Ground Brewery and Moon Dog Craft Brewery and Mollie Rose. Picture this: strolling down Smith Street, watching the colourful scene, friendly people, aromas of culturally diverse foods and good tunes. The vibe is electric, and you'll meet some interesting folks along the way. It's like the grunge scene of the '90s found a new home here!

Dog friendly

And here's the kicker – Melbourne's dog-friendly bars are off the leash! Literally! Bring your furry friend along for the adventure because this city knows how to welcome four-legged pals. Places like The Local Taphouse in St Kilda or The Terminus Hotel in Abbotsford not only serve up top-notch brews but also roll out the welcome mat for your canine companions. Picture sipping on a cold one while your pup makes new friends – it's the perfect way to wind down after a day of exploring this vibrant city. Melbourne truly has something for everyone.
